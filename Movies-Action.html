Description:The Sam Raimi Spider-Man trilogy — Spider-Man, Spider-Man 2, and Spider-Man 3 — follows Peter Parker as he grows from a shy student into a confident superhero. Across the trilogy, he balances personal struggles, love, and responsibility while battling powerful villains, learning that being Spider-Man requires sacrifice and courage.

Description:The Amazing Spider-Man reimagines Peter Parker’s origin as he investigates the mystery of his parents’ disappearance. After gaining spider-like powers, he becomes a hero while facing dangerous enemies and learning the heavy responsibility that comes with his abilities.

Description:The MCU Spider-Man trilogy — Spider-Man: Homecoming, Spider-Man: Far From Home, and Spider-Man: No Way Home — follows a young Peter Parker mentored by the Avengers as he grows into an independent hero. He faces increasingly dangerous threats, navigates high school life, and eventually confronts multiverse chaos that tests his identity and sense of responsibility.

Description:The Iron Man trilogy — Iron Man, Iron Man 2, and Iron Man 3 — centers on Tony Stark, a genius inventor who builds advanced armored suits to fight global threats. Over the series, Tony evolves from a self-focused billionaire into a hero willing to risk everything to protect others.

Description:The Avengers brings together Earth’s mightiest heroes — Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye — as they unite to stop a massive alien invasion. The film highlights teamwork, sacrifice, and the power of heroes working together to save the world.
